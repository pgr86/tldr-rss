TLDR RSS

RSS and HTML feeds are generated on request and cached by Vercel for 4 hours. Try /feed.rss, /tech.rss, or /tech.html.

Supported feeds
  • feed.rss
  • tech.rss
  • ai.rss
  • crypto.rss
  • product.rss
  • design.rss
  • dev.rss
  • devops.rss
  • marketing.rss
  • data.rss
  • fintech.rss

Optional environment variable: MAX_DAYS controls how far back the feed generator looks.
